# Startup Prompts

## Startup Idea Generator

Prompt:

Act as a startup advisor.

Generate 10 startup ideas for the following niche.

Niche: [insert niche]

For each idea include:
- problem
- solution
- target market
- monetization strategy

---

## SaaS Product Idea Generator

Prompt:

Act as a SaaS founder.

Generate 10 SaaS product ideas that solve real-world problems.

Include:
- target users
- core features
- pricing idea

---

## Startup Validation Prompt

Prompt:

Act as a startup strategist.

Analyze the following business idea.

Idea: [insert idea]

Provide:
- market demand
- potential competitors
- risks
- suggestions for improvement

## Business Model Builder

Prompt:

Act as a startup consultant.

Create a business model for the following idea.

Idea: [insert idea]
